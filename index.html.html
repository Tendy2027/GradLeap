Platform Features

Everything you need
before you arrive

GradLeap brings together all the information, tools, and people you need to start your tertiary journey with confidence.

🏠
Accommodation Finder

Browse verified accommodation options near your institution. See exact costs, distance from campus, transport routes, and what to budget for, to avoid any surprises.

📖
First-Year Study Hub

Access curated study resources tailored to your diploma or degree. Know what to expect in your first semester and hit the ground running.

💰
Bursary & Scholarship Matcher

Discover funding opportunities you qualify for. We help you find bursaries, track deadlines, and understand your options - whether you're fully funded or managing debt.

🤝
Student Community

Connect with other incoming students, current students, and alumni at your institution. You'll never feel alone on your first day again.

🗺️
Campus & Course Guide

Get a head start on navigating your campus. Learn what your course involves, who teaches it, and what past students say about year one.

📋
Fee & Budget Planner

Understand the full cost of your qualification - tuition, registration, accommodation, and transport - so you can plan financially before you commit.
